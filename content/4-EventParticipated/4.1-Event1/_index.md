**Summary Report:** “Kick-off AWS FCJ Workforce – FPTU OJT Fall 2025 provided an overview of the program, AWS tools, and OJT expectations for students.”  

**Event Objectives**  
- Introduce the AWS FCJ Workforce program and the OJT Fall 2025 plan  
- Guide students on OJT workflow, timeline, and key activities  
- Set expectation for skills development and learning outcomes  
- Provide information on AWS tools and platforms to be used  

**Speakers / Organizers**  
- Kha Van – Program Coordinator, FPT University  

**Key Highlights**  
- Event held on **Saturday, September 6, 2025, 8:30 - 12:00 GMT+7** at **Bitexco Financial Tower, District 1, Ho Chi Minh City**  
- Overview of the program: OJT structure, AWS modules, timeline, and key milestones  
- Introduction to platforms, tools, and guidance for AWS environment setup for students  
- Direct interaction between students and mentors about learning expectations and effective study methods  

**Event Experience**  
- Students gained a clear view of the OJT journey with AWS and the skills to develop  
- Opportunities to exchange ideas with mentors and support teams, enhancing teamwork and soft skills  
- Understanding how to prepare, plan, and effectively participate in AWS Hands-on labs, workshops, and final assessments  

**Lessons Learned / Takeaways**  
- Clear understanding of objectives and structure of AWS FCJ Workforce Fall 2025 OJT  
- Preparedness regarding tools, learning environment, and AWS resources  
- Set expectations for skills, progress, and outcomes after completing the OJT
